How it come from

Hi, Everyone. Welcome to BlackCarDriver.cn, I am XianJinRong, a computer major student In Guangzhou University. I got a idea in the May of 2019 when I see many useable and very new things were abandon around the garbage when graduates were going to leave there place, Most of those thing were just be throw away wastefully , Which made me fell so pity. I have been though that we need a better tool to promote our communication and promote the cyclic utilization of our unused goods. It is very meaningful and it is why it website come form!

What is it

It site is a platform for our schoolmate to show what they want to sell and for those who want to save some money by buy second-hand goods instead of expensive new one. But in face, You can do more, It is also a community for you to make friends, and show your production such as your program work, your painting, handcraft etc. Sure, you can share it for free if you want. You are welcome to register a account to sign in and upload what you want to sell or share, and see what other schoolmate are sharing. If you are interesting in some of the goods, you can connect author with the email, qq, phone number hi/she leave or even just send he/she a message in it site directly.
